# Topic[^1]
\begin{theorem}
\label{thm: general type}
Assume that $k$ is a finite field of characteristic $p \ge 5$. Let $X$ be a projective algebraic surface over $k$ with $p_g = K_X^2 = 1$. If $K_X$ is ample, then $X$ satisfies the Tate conjecture.
\end{theorem}
