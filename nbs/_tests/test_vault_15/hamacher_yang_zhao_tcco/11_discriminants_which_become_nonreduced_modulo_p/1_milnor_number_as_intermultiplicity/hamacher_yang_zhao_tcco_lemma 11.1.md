# Topic[^1]
\begin{lemma}
\label{lem: image of P(N)}
The discriminant locus $D \subseteq \check{\mathbb{P}}$ of the morphism $\mathcal{X} \to \check{\mathbb{P}}$ is simply the image of the projection $\mathbb{P}(N) \to \check{\mathbb{P}}$.
\end{lemma}
