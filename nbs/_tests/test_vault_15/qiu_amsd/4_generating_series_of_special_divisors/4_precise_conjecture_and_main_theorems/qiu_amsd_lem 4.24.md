# Topic[^1]
\begin{lem} \label{equiva} For $h\in K_{\Lambda}$,       the     ``right translation by $h$" automorphism on ${\mathcal {X}}_{K}$  
sends $z(g,  \phi)_{{\mathfrak{e}},a}^{{\overline\cL}}$ to $z(g, \omega(h)\phi)_{{\mathfrak{e}},a}^{{\overline\cL}}$. 
\end{lem}
