# Topic[^1]
\begin{lem}[{\cite[PROPOSITION5.10]{Kud97}\cite[Corollary 3.4]{Liu}}]\label{yespull} We have $\pi_{K,K'}^*Z_t(\phi)_{K'}=Z_t(\phi)_K$ and $\pi_{K,K'}^*L_{K
'}=L_K$.
 In particular, $\pi_{K,K'}^*z(\phi)_{K'}=z(\phi)_K$.
\end{lem}
