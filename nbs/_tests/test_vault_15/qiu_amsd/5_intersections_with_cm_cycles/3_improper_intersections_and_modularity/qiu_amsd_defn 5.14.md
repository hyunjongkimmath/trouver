# Topic[^1]
\begin{defn}\label{regdef}For a finite place $v$ of $F$, we say that $\phi_v$ is ${\mathbb {W}}_v$-regular if it   is supported outside  the orthogonal complement  ${\mathbb {W}}_v^\perp=V^\sharp(E_{v})$.
\end{defn}
