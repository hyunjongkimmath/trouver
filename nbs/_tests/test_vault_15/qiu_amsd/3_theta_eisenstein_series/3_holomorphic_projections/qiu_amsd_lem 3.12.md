# Topic[^1]
\begin{lem}\label{wr} For   a place $v$ of $F$ and   $k\in K^{\max}_v$,  we have $E(s,g,\omega(k)\phi)=E(s,gk,\phi)$. The same relation holds for  $C(s,g,\phi)$,  ${E\theta}(s,g,\phi) $ and  ${E\theta}'_{{\mathrm{chol}}}(0,g,\phi).$\end{lem}
