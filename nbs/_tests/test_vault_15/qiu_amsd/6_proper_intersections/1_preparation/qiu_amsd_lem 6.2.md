# Topic[^1]
\begin{lem} [{\cite[Lemma 9.2]{YZ}}] \label {YZ7.41}
Assume that $v\in {\mathfrak{Ram}}-{\mathfrak{Ram}}_{E/F}$.
Then $\phi_v'$     extends to a Schwartz function on  $ V(E_v)$  such that 
$c(1,\phi_v)-2\phi_v'(0)=2\log|D_{F_v}|_{v}$.   \end{lem}
