# Topic[^1]
\begin{lem}\label{smoothadm}
Assume that ${\mathcal {M}}$ is smooth over ${\mathrm{Spec}} {\mathcal {O}}$. Then $B_{\mathcal {L}}^1({\mathcal {M}})=0$. In particular, 
for $X\in Z^1(M)$,  $X^{\mathrm{zar}}$ is the normalized admissible extension.

\end{lem}
