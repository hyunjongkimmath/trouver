# Topic[^1]
\begin{defn} The  group **${\mathcal G}$** is called  the Weil restriction
of  scalars  from  $K$ to  $\mathbb{Q}$,  of  the  group  $G$. It  is  denoted
**${\mathcal G}=R_{K/\mathbb{Q}}(G)$**.
\end{defn}
