# Ordinary abelian variety[^1]
Let $A$ be an [[lombardo_av_1.3|abelian variety]] over a field $K$ of characteristic $p$. 

We say that $A$ is **ordinary** if $A[p](\bar{K})$ has order $p^{\operatorname{dim} A}$.
